Privacy

Smart Wake is designed so there is nothing to collect and nowhere to send it.

The short version: your sleep data is read from Apple Health on your device, used on your device, and never leaves your device. No tracking, no analytics, no account, no servers.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

With your permission, Smart Wake reads sleep data from Apple Health to find a lighter sleep moment inside your wake window. That data is processed entirely on your iPhone and Apple Watch. It is never uploaded, shared, sold, or used for advertising — Smart Wake operates no servers it could send it to. You can revoke access at any time in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices, and the backup alarm keeps working exactly as before.

No tracking, no account

  • No account or sign-in — the app never asks who you are.

  • No data leaves your devices. Alarm times, windows and the morning recap are stored locally.

Making sure the alarm can always sound

Smart Wake checks these at setup and warns you on the alarm screen if anything would get in the way. If an alarm was quieter or later than expected, walk through this list:

  • Notifications allowed: Settings → Notifications → Smart Wake → Allow Notifications on, with Sounds enabled.
  • Focus modes: if you use Sleep Focus or Do Not Disturb, add Smart Wake to the allowed apps (Settings → Focus → your Focus → Allowed Notifications).
  • Volume: keep the ringer volume up. The alarm uses the ringer, not the media volume.
  • Keep the app installed: deleting or offloading the app removes its alarms. iOS offloading can be disabled per app in Settings.

The smart window didn't trigger early

  • Wear the Watch to bed with wrist detection on (Watch app → Passcode → Wrist Detection).
  • Health permission: check Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Smart Wake and confirm sleep data access is granted.
  • Window length: confirm a wake window is set on the alarm. With no window — or no lighter moment found inside it — the alarm simply fires at your chosen time, as designed.
  • An early start is a bonus, never a requirement: the backup alarm at your chosen time does not depend on the Watch at all.
